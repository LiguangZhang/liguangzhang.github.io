64 位上增加了状态寄存器, 32 位先忽略不看

  • mstateen0 mstateen1 mstateen2 mstateen3
  • sstateen0 sstateen1 sstateen2 sstateen3
  • hstateen0 hstateen1 hstateen2 hstateen3

主要目的, status csr 位数不够用, 为了将来的扩展, 添加了这些状态寄存器, 来控制系统行为.

和虚拟化有关的为

mstateen0

(允许 M-mode 下访问的寄存器控制)

  • bit 58 IMSIC state of the Advanced Interrupt Architecture (AIA)
    all IMSIC state, including CSRs stopei and vstopei
    Siph, sieh, stopi, hidelegh, hvien/hvienh, hviph, Hvictl, hviprio1/hviprio1h, hviprio2/hviprio2h, vsiph, vsieh, and vstopi
    This bit does not affect the behavior or accessibility of hypervisor CSRs hgeip and hgeie, or field VGEIN of hstatus.
  • bit 59 Most other registers of the AIA
    • Bit 59 controls access to AIA CSRs sieh, siph, hidelegh, hvien/hvienh, hviph, hvictl, hviprio1/hviprio1h,
      Hviprio2/hviprio2h, vsieh, and vsiph, as well as the supervisor-level iprio array accessed through siselect + sireg.
  • bit 60 siselect, vsiselect of the AIA
  • bit 62 henvcfg/henvcfgh, senvcfg

sstateen0

(允许 HS-mode 下访问的寄存器控制位)
同上

hstateen0

(允许 vs-mode 下访问的 aia 相关的 csr)

  • bit 58 IMSIC state of the AIA
    • 58 位为 0 时, 等同于设置 hstatus. VGEIN=0, 所有的 aia 的寄存器不可访问 siph, sieh, stopi, hidelegh, hvien/hvienh, hviph,
      Hvictl, hviprio1/hviprio1h, hviprio2/hviprio2h, vsiph, vsieh, and vstopi, siselect + sireg 不可访问
  • bit 60 siselect of the AIA
    • 控制 VS-mode 下 sislect sireg (实际上为 vsislect vsireg)
  • bit 59
    • 控制 VS-mode 下 stopi (实际上为 vstopi)的访问权限
  • bit 62 senvcfg
